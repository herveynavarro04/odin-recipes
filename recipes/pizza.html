Salami Pizza Recipe

Salami Pizza Final Result

A salami pizza features a golden, crispy crust topped with tangy pizza sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and savory slices of salami. The addition of black olives provides a burst of briny flavor, creating a perfect balance of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced salami
  • Sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough.
  2. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Arrange sliced salami and black olives on top.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until crust is golden.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
